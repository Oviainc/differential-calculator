Interest Differential Calculator

Enter the total amount of the mortgage or loan you want to analyze. For example, $120,000.
Enter the higher interest rate of the two options you are comparing. For example, 5%.
Enter the lower interest rate of the two options you are comparing. For example, 3%.
Enter the number of months you want to analyze the interest differential for. For example, 3 months.

Example Calculation

Let's say you have the following values:

  • Principal Amount: $120,000
  • Higher Interest Rate: 5%
  • Lower Interest Rate: 3%
  • Number of Months: 3

The Interest Differential would be calculated as:

        Interest Differential = Principal × (Higher Rate - Lower Rate) × (Months / 12)
        = $120,000 × ((5%/12) - (3%/12)) × 3
        = $120,000 × (0.00416667 - 0.0025) × 3
        = $120,000 × 0.00166667 × 3
        = $600

So, in this example, you would save $600 over 3 months by choosing the lower interest rate.
