Introduction

Welcome to the Mortgage Prepayment Penalty Calculator! This tool is designed to help you protect your financial interests by allowing you to evaluate the potential penalties associated with paying off your mortgage early or making additional payments. It calculates two primary methods for determining your prepayment penalty: Three Months' Interest and Interest Rate Differential (IRD).

The primary goal of this calculator is to ensure you don't end up paying a higher penalty than necessary. By comparing both penalty options, you can choose the more economical alternative, saving you money and making informed financial decisions regarding your mortgage obligations.
