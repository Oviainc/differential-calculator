Introduction

Welcome to the BMO Mortgage Prepayment Penalty Calculator! This tool is designed to help you understand the potential penalties associated with paying off your mortgage early or making additional payments. The calculator evaluates two primary methods for determining your prepayment penalty: Three Months' Interest and Interest Rate Differential (IRD). By entering your mortgage details, you can quickly assess which method results in a higher penalty, empowering you to make informed financial decisions regarding your mortgage obligations.
